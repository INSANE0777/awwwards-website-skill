## 1. THE "ANATOMY" OF AN ELITE PROMPT
A common mistake is asking for "A cool hero section." To get SOTY quality, your prompt must include **Motion Physics** and **Technical Stack constraints**.

### The "Awwwards Alpha" Prompt Template:
> "Generate a [Archetype] hero section using [Tech Stack: Next.js + Tailwind v4 + GSAP]. 
> **Visuals:** [Palette], [Typography: Instrument Sans], [Grain: 3% Overlay].
> **Motion:** Implement a 'Liquid Fluid' background shader. Title should stagger from y:100 with 'expo.out' easing.
> **Interaction:** On mouse move, distort the background mesh by 0.2 intensity. Lens cursor with 12vw blur."

## 3. THE "AI-DIRECTOR" WORKFLOW
Instead of fixing bugs, **Refine the Intent**.

1. **Step 1: Structural Gen.** "Build the layout and typography foundation."
2. **Step 2: Motion Injection.** "Now, wrap the hero text in a GSAP SplitText timeline."
3. **Step 3: Texture Pass.** "Add the paper grain noise and the chromatic aberration post-processing."
4. **Step 4: Edge Case Polish.** "Implement the Adaptive Quality Scaling (AQS) for potato devices."
