# Modern Component Ecosystems (2026)
## Integrating ReactBits, 21st.dev, and Beyond

High-end creative development in 2026 focuses on **Customization over Creation**. We use elite registries to source foundations, then "Awwward-ify" them.

---

## 1. THE TOP 4 REGISTRIES
| Provider | Vibe | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **ReactBits** | Immersive / Creative | Shaders, Particle Backgrounds, 3D Interactions. |
| **21st.dev** | Atomic / Functional | High-performance buttons, navigations, and specific UI atoms. |
| **Aceternity UI** | Sleek / SaaS | 3D Cards, Animated Modals, Modern Layouts. |
| **Magic UI** | Flashy / Marketing | Landing page "Hero" moments and over-the-top 3D effects. |

---

## 2. THE "AWWWARDS" WORKFLOW
Don't just `npx` and leave it. Follow the **10/90 Rule**:
- **10% Foundation:** Copy the component from the registry (e.g., a "Shader Reveal" from ReactBits).
- **90% Customization:**
    - Change the easing from `framer-motion` defaults to custom GSAP `expo.inOut`.
    - Inject custom GLSL noise into the shader source.
    - Tie the component's internal state to the site's global `Lenis` scroll position.

---

## 3. COMPONENT SOURCING STRATEGY
1. **Identify the "Awe" moment:** Does the Hero need a particle field? (Source: ReactBits Backgrounds).
2. **Atomic Sourcing:** Need a magnetic button? (Source: 21st.dev).
3. **Integration:** Bring the code into `components/creative/` and immediate refactor to use the site's primary color variables and typography tokens.
