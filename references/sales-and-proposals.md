# Sales & Proposals for Creative Development
## How to sell Awwwards-level websites for $15k–$50k+

Selling premium digital experiences is different from selling standard web design. You are selling **immersion, status, and storytelling**, not just a tool for business.

---

## 1. THE PRICING TIERS (2025 Market Rates)

| Tier | Price Range | Scope | Ideal Client |
|---|---|---|---|
| **The Boutique Folio** | $8k – $15k | Single-page immersive portfolio, custom animations | Individual artists, directors |
| **The Brand Experience** | $15k – $35k | Multi-page, 3D/WebGL elements, custom sound | Luxury brands, tech startups |
| **The SOTY Contender** | $40k – $100k+ | Full 3D environment, complex GLSL, deep narrative | Enterprise brand launches |

---

## 2. THE VALUE-BASED PROPOSAL STRUCTURE

Don't send a list of features. Send a **Vision Document**.

### A. The Challenge
Define the client's current status (e.g., "The brand feels static in a dynamic market").

### B. The Transformation
Describe the emotional state of the visitor (e.g., "We will create a 'digital sanctuary' where users don't just browse, they inhabit").

### C. The Signature Moments
Propose 2–3 "Screenshot-worthy" moments (e.g., "The Liquid Transition", "The 3D Hero").

### D. The Technical Assurance
Briefly mention the high-end stack (GSAP, Three.js, Lenis) and why it matters for **retention and brand perception**.

---

## 3. "THE REVEAL" PRESENTATION STRATEGY

Never just email a link. 

1. **The Teaser:** Send a 15-second screen recording of the loader and first interaction (The "Hook").
2. **The Walkthrough:** Host a meeting to show the site. Explain the **Design Intention** behind the motion.
3. **The Proof:** Show a Lighthouse performance report. Prove that "Beautiful" doesn't mean "Slow".

---

## 4. HANDLING REVISIONS (The "Museum" Rule)

Awwwards-level sites are like curated exhibits. Frequent "client tweaks" can break the rhythm.

- **Soft Rule:** Client controls the CONTENT and the GOAL.
- **Hard Rule:** Studio controls the MOTION and the COMPOSITION.
- **Protocol:** Explain *why* a requested change would break the "Physics of the Brand".

---

## 5. THE AWWWARDS SUBMISSION STRATEGY

Don't just hit submit. Win the developer award too.

1. **Submission Video:** Create a high-quality 60-second walkthrough video. Use professional screen recording with a custom cursor.
2. **The "Behind the Scenes":** Create a case study page (like this one) explaining the technical challenges (GLSL, performance). Judges LOVE this.
3. **Timing:** Submit on a Tuesday or Wednesday for maximum visibility during the "Site of the Day" judging cycle.

---

## 6. UPSSELLING RETAINERS

Creative sites need love. Sell a **"Performance & Motion" Retainer**:
- Monthly GLSL optimization.
- Interactive content updates.
- Seasonal "Signature Moment" refreshes (e.g., a "Snow" shader for winter).
